Eric Migicovsky, Founder & CEO of Pebble

About

Eric Migicovsky is a systems design engineer and the founder of Pebble Technology, the company behind the Pebble smartwatch.

After incubating his idea for the smartwatch in the Y Combinator program, he helped create the largest Kickstarter project to date. As of July 2013, Pebble has sold over 275,000 smartwatches.
